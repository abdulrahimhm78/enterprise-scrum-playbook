# Scrum Master (Enterprise Role Definition)

## Accountability
The Scrum Master is accountable for enabling effective
delivery, improving team maturity, and reinforcing
Agile discipline across the team.

## Core Responsibilities
- Facilitate Scrum events with clear purpose
- Remove impediments and escalate blockers
- Coach the team on Agile principles and practices
- Enable transparency through metrics and visibility
- Protect the team from unmanaged disruptions

## What This Role Is Not
- A project administrator
- A task assigner
- A status reporter

## Success Indicators
- Predictable delivery outcomes
- High team engagement
- Continuous improvement adoption
