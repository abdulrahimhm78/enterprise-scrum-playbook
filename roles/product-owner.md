# Product Owner (Enterprise Role Definition)

## Accountability
The Product Owner is accountable for maximizing
product value by managing and prioritizing the backlog
based on business outcomes and stakeholder needs.

## Core Responsibilities
- Own and prioritize the product backlog
- Define and clarify acceptance criteria
- Collaborate with stakeholders and the delivery team
- Balance business priorities with technical constraints
- Accept completed work based on agreed criteria

## What This Role Is Not
- A project manager
- A task assigner
- A proxy decision-maker without authority

## Success Indicators
- Clear and actionable backlog
- Well-defined acceptance criteria
- Strong alignment between business and delivery teams
