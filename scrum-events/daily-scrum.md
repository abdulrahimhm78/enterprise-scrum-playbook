# Daily Scrum (Execution Alignment)

## Purpose
The Daily Scrum is a team-owned event used to inspect progress
toward the sprint goal and adapt the plan for the next 24 hours.

## Focus Areas
- Progress toward the sprint goal
- Blockers and impediments
- Coordination between team members
- Immediate plan adjustments

## Good Practices
- Keep the discussion outcome-focused
- Raise blockers early
- Take deep discussions offline

## Common Mistakes
- Status reporting to the Scrum Master
- Solving problems without the right people
- Ignoring blockers until late in the sprint
