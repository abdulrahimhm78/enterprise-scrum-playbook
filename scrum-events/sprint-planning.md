
# Sprint Planning (Enterprise Standard)

## Purpose
Sprint Planning establishes a shared commitment between the team and
stakeholders on what will be delivered in the sprint and how the work
will be executed.

## Inputs
- Prioritized and refined backlog
- Team capacity (including PTO and support work)
- Identified dependencies and risks
- Definition of Ready

## Expected Outcomes
- Clear sprint goal
- Realistic sprint commitment
- Visibility into risks and dependencies
- Shared understanding across the team

## Common Mistakes
- Overcommitting due to external pressure
- Ignoring capacity constraints
- Treating planning as a status meeting
